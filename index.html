European Interest Group on ANARI
(EU-IGA)
A European Initiative of academics and industry partners to aid in the standardization of 3D rendering in computer graphics and scientific visualization, by use of the open standard ANARI.

Project Mission
EU-IGA is a (for now loosely) coordinated initiative of partners primarily from the EU who seek to have an influence on the standardization of 3D rendering in computer graphics and scientific visualization. The interest group promotes Khronos' open standard ANARI (https://www.khronos.org/anari), which provides a common API for renderers and visualization apps to interface with. As the Khronos working group is primarily driven by industry players from the US; EU-IGA is a European initiative whose mission is to represent the interests of academics and industry partners from the EU in this open standard. EU-IGA closely collaborates with the Khronos ANARI working group members.
